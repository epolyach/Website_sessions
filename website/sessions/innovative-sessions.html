Innovative Sessions

An innovation session is a fast and powerful way to shake up your team's mindset and uncover new solutions where there used to be only limits or "unsolvable" problems. Dynamic, precise, and with a tangible impact.

For leaders, it's a one-day reboot of the team's strategic thinking — fast, inspiring, and ending with concrete ideas ready for implementation.

For example, when Reebok faced recurring theft at their factories, instead of tightening controls, they applied inventive thinking — producing right and left shoes at separate plants. Theft dropped instantly, and the idea became a legendary example of creative problem-solving in action.
