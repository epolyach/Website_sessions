Transformation Sessions

Making lasting change possible through guided self-discovery, mindset shifts, and purposeful action.

Understand the psychology of feedback and learn key models (COIN, Avery, Radical Candor). Gain practical tools to increase team communication effectiveness.

2. Mindset Shift

Experience all kinds of innovative sessions where teams tackle "unsolvable" tasks. Unlock creativity, reframe challenges, and generate breakthrough solutions.

You can also discover our Mindset Shift sessions powered by LEGO® Serious Play. These workshops use hands-on building and storytelling techniques to help teams visualize new perspectives, boost engagement, and co-create actionable strategies. 3. Retrospective – New Rules of Work & Team Agreement

Use frameworks like Mad/Sad/Glad to surface problems, reflect on past ways of working, and co-create actionable solutions. Establish clear team agreements and improve collaboration.

4. All-in-One Session: Mindset Shift + New Rules of Work + Team Collaboration

A single session drives mindset transformation, practical behavior change, and stronger team alignment, leaving participants with concrete tools, new ways of working, and elevated collaboration.
