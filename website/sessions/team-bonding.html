Sociometry

Sociometry is a powerful method for visualizing and understanding the hidden patterns of relationships and communication within teams. Through structured exercises, team members discover their natural connections, identify potential conflicts, and find new ways to collaborate.

Key Benefits:

  • Reveals unspoken team dynamics
  • Identifies informal leaders and influencers
  • Strengthens trust and psychological safety
  • Improves communication patterns

Management 3.0 Practices

Management 3.0 offers a modern toolkit for empowering teams and creating environments where people can do their best work. These interactive practices focus on motivation, delegation, and continuous improvement.

Popular Practices Include:

  • Delegation Poker: Clarifying decision-making authorities in a fun, interactive way
  • Moving Motivators: Understanding what truly drives each team member
  • Celebration Grid: Learning from both successes and failures
  • Kudo Cards: Building a culture of appreciation and recognition

Session Format

Sessions are typically 2-4 hours and can be conducted in-person or online. Each session is tailored to your team's specific needs and challenges, combining interactive exercises with reflection and action planning.
